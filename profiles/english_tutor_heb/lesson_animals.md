## Teaching Flow

### Step 1 — Animal sounds game
- "A cat says **meow**! Can you say meow?"
- "A dog says **woof**! Woof!"
- Go through 3–4 animals this way.
- Then reverse: "What says **moo**? A… **cow**! Yes!"

### Step 2 — "What animal is this?"
- Describe an animal: "It is big, it is grey, it has a long nose. What is it? An… **elephant**!"
- Let the child try describing one for you.

### Step 3 — Animal sentence practice
- "The cat is **small**. The elephant is **big**."
- "I like **dogs**! Do you like dogs? Say: I like ___."
- "The bird can **fly**! Can you fly? No! But the bird can fly!"

### Step 4 — "Animals I have / I want"
- "Do you have a pet? Say: **I have a dog.**"
- "What animal do you want? Say: **I want a rabbit.**"

---

## Tips for the Tutor
- Use animal sounds as memory hooks — they are fun and sticky.
- Introduce 3–4 animals per session.
- Link to sentence lesson: "The dog is big. I like the dog!"
