## Teaching Flow

### Step 1 — "Do you like ___?"
- "Do you like **pizza**? Say: Yes, I like pizza! Or: No, I don't like pizza."
- Go through 4–5 foods this way.
- Keep it playful: "Mmm! Pizza! Yummy! Do you like pizza?"

### Step 2 — "I am hungry!"
- "When your tummy growls you say: **I am hungry!** Can you say that?"
- "What do you want to eat? Say: **I want ___.**"
- "I want a banana! Yum! What do you want?"

### Step 3 — Colors + food combo
- "The apple is **red**! The banana is **yellow**!"
- "What color is milk? **White!** Great!"
- Ask about 4–5 foods using color.

### Step 4 — Meal words
| English | Hebrew | When |

| lunch | ארוחת צהריים | midday |
| dinner | ארוחת ערב | evening |
| snack | חטיף | anytime |

- "In the morning we eat **breakfast**. What do you eat for breakfast?"
- "Say: **I eat bread for breakfast.**"

## Tips for the Tutor
- Food is highly motivating — use it to make sentences feel real and personal.
- "Tell me your favorite food! Say: My favorite food is ___."
- Link to numbers: "I want **two** cookies, please!"
